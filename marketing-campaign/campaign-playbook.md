# Own Your Web — Paid Social Campaign Playbook

## Campaign goal

Generate qualified applications from small-business owners who need consistent social media management. All ads direct to:

`https://innergclaw.github.io/smm-service-tier-1/?utm_source={{platform}}&utm_medium=paid_social&utm_campaign=oyw_system_launch&utm_content={{creative}}#apply`

## Offer

**AI-Powered Social Media Management — $450/month. No setup fee.**

Core proof points: Instagram and Facebook management, 12 monthly posts, professional graphics, captions, a planned posting schedule, approval workflow, one revision round per batch, and monthly analytics. AI supports the workflow; human review and client approval remain part of the system.

## Audience

Primary buyers: owners and operators of salons, beauty brands, med spas, real-estate businesses, contractors, fitness brands, nonprofits, coaches, consultants, and local service businesses.

### Prospecting ad sets

1. **Broad local business owners:** ages 24–55, selected service area or U.S.-wide if delivery is fully remote. Avoid over-narrowing during the first learning period.
2. **Business-interest test:** entrepreneurship, small business, digital marketing, social media marketing, business software, and relevant vertical interests.
3. **Vertical test:** one ad set each for beauty/wellness, professional services, and home/local services. Match the first line of copy to the vertical.

### Retargeting

- Website visitors from the last 30 days.
- Instagram and Facebook engagers from the last 30–90 days.
- Video viewers who watched at least 50%.
- Application-page visitors who did not submit.

Exclude recent applicants and active clients from acquisition ad sets.

## Funnel structure

### Phase 1 — Attention (days 1–7)

Run all three creative concepts with the same conversion event and broad audience. Optimize for completed applications if tracking volume supports it; otherwise begin with landing-page views while the conversion signal develops.

### Phase 2 — Qualification (days 8–14)

Pause obvious underperformers only after they receive a fair share of spend. Keep the strongest hook, then test one variable at a time: opening line, visual colorway, or 15-second versus 30-second voiceover.

### Phase 3 — Retargeting (day 10 onward)

Use the transparent offer creative and a direct reminder: “Your content system is ready when you are.” Send traffic directly to the application form.

## Practical starting budget

Suggested test budget: **$30–$60/day total for 14 days**, split roughly 60% Meta prospecting, 25% TikTok prospecting, and 15% cross-platform retargeting once the retargeting pools are large enough. This is a testing recommendation, not a guaranteed result. If the budget is lower, test Meta first and add TikTok after a winning message emerges.

## Measurement

Track: thumb-stop/3-second view rate, outbound CTR, landing-page view rate, application-start rate, completed applications, cost per application, qualified-application rate, and booked-client rate. Do not judge success on likes alone.

Use platform pixels plus UTMs. Confirm the form-submission success state can fire a conversion event before launch. Meta Pixel/Conversions API and TikTok Pixel/Events API are placeholders until connected; no raw customer data should be placed in ad creative or URLs.

## Launch checklist

- Confirm the landing page and form work on mobile.
- Replace `{{platform}}` and `{{creative}}` macros with each platform’s supported URL parameters or fixed values.
- Install and test conversion tracking.
- Add an AI-generated-content disclosure on TikTok when AI voice/audio is used.
- Use only licensed music and a commercially cleared AI voice.
- Keep text inside the vertical safe zone.
- Verify offer details match the landing page exactly.
- Proofread captions and confirm every CTA opens the application form.
