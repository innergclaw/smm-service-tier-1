## Recommended rollout sequence

1. Hero screenshot: “Your business deserves a content system, not random posting.”
2. Offer screenshot: “Twelve posts, approvals, reporting, and one clear monthly price.”
3. Client dashboard: “Know exactly what is planned, waiting, approved, and published.”
4. Content calendar: “Review every post before it goes live.”
5. Analytics: “See what performed and what to improve next month.”
6. Admin views: “A real client workflow behind every deliverable.”
